ABOUT US

We follow the development of the products we have chosen for you from the seed stage.

Deren Fresh founded in 2020 by RIDVAN TAŞDELEN in Fethiye, MUĞLA. Our main export products are tomatoes, cucumbers, zucchini, peppers, eggplants, citrus fruits, pomegranates, peaches, nectarines, cherries, and figs. Exporting the majority of the products produced in Turkey and grown in accordance with the standards; is a supplier and exporter company that works in partnership with the leading companies in the sector. It operates for the foreign market and mainly carries out export activities in Russia and European countries.
